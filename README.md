# E-Commerce Customer Analytics & Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project performs end-to-end data analytics on an e-commerce dataset containing over 100,000 orders.  
The goal is to extract business insights related to customer behavior, revenue performance, product trends, and delivery logistics.

The project demonstrates a full analytics workflow including:
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Customer segmentation using RFM analysis
- Revenue and product performance analysis
- Delivery logistics evaluation
- Interactive Power BI business dashboard

---

## Dataset
Dataset used: **Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset (Olist)**  
It contains information about customers, orders, products, sellers, payments, and reviews.

Total records analyzed: **100,000+ orders**

## Key Analytics Performed

### 1. Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)
Customers were segmented using Recency, Frequency, and Monetary metrics.

Customer groups identified:
- Champions
- Loyal Customers
- Regular Customers
- At-Risk Customers

This segmentation helps businesses target marketing campaigns effectively.

---

### 2. Revenue Analysis
Key insights:
- Total marketplace revenue analyzed across multiple product categories
- Identification of highest performing product categories
- Analysis of top revenue generating sellers

---

### 3. Product Performance
Analysis revealed the most profitable product categories and purchasing trends across customers.

Top revenue generating categories include:
- Bed & Bath
- Health & Beauty
- Sports & Leisure
- Computer Accessories

---

### 4. Delivery & Logistics Performance
Delivery time and delay metrics were calculated to evaluate logistics efficiency.

Key metrics analyzed:
- Average delivery time
- Late delivery rate
- Seller delivery performance
- Regional delivery trends

---

### 5. Customer Behavior Insights
Customer purchasing behavior analysis revealed:

- Majority of customers make **one-time purchases**
- A small segment of customers contributes **a large portion of revenue**
- High-value customers generate disproportionate marketplace value
